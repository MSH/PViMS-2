When to complete this form: This form should be completed for every patient at Treatment Initiation – the interview at which DTG based regimen is prescribed for the first time and the patient is enrolled into the active monitoring programme.

Patient participation:  Patients may be enrolled if they are beginning treatment with DTG based regimen for the first time (i.e. treatment naïve or are switched from NVP based regimen). It is important that monitoring begins at the commencement of therapy.

Treatment site: Please provide the name of the site where patient is being enrolled into the active monitoring program.

Patient ASM Code:  Each patient enrolled should have a unique active safety monitoring (ASM) code. The coding system should be agreed upon and standardized across all sites by the country. Please use the patient’s ASM code as the unique identifier for that patient. 

Tick boxes (o):  Where there are tick boxes, please answer by placing a tick þ in the appropriate box.

Patient Details

Patient name: Please provide patient’s Surname and given name (s) or initials. Hospital number: Record the file number used to identify this patient in your clinic. 

Date of birth: If date of birth is not known, record the patient’s age (or estimated age).

Sex: Please provide patient’s sex at birth. Pregnancy: For all female patients indicate pregnancy status with LMP and estimated gestational age if pregnant. 

Address:  Provide a traceable address for patient in the vent that home visit may become necessary. 

Phone number: Provide patient’s mobile number in the event that follow up by phone becomes necessary.

Medical history prior to commencement of DTG based regimen

Alcohol/smoking and other substance abuse: Indicate if the patient has a history of smoking, use of alcohol or other substances. 

Medical conditions: Please indicate if patient had or has any of the listed and/or other medical conditions, and if so, indicate if it was in the past or still ongoing.

Laboratory tests

Record the results (including units) of the latest laboratory tests taken prior to commencement of DTG based regimen. A list of commonly performed tests has been provided; the details of any additional tests may be recorded in the rows marked ‘Other’.

Medicines taken in last 1 month

Medicines & traditional/herbal medicines: Record the details of any prescription or over-the counter medicines and any traditional medicines, herbal remedies or health supplements taken at any time during the past one month. Include the start and stop dates, indication for use, dosage and frequency in the columns provides. If patient is still taking the medicine tick continuing. If a medicine is given as a fixed dose combination (FDC), or in a co-blister pack, record it as a single medicine with the number of dosage forms (DF) given. 

Medicines prescribed at this visit

Record the names of the ART medicines or fixed dose combination (FDC) regimen and other medicines prescribed at this visit. The full details of these medicines will also be recorded on the Follow up Form (Form B) at the next visit, when the medicines have actually been taken.

Reasons for use of DTG based regimen

Indicate the reason(s) for the patient being placed on DTG based regimen at this visit by ticking the most appropriate option

Date of follow up visit

Assign to the patient the date to return to the site for follow up visit. This should be approximately one month after the treatment initiation visit. Record the date in the space provided

Details of reporter

Provide name, signature and profession of the reporter. This will typically be the person enrolling the patient into the active monitoring program. Indicate the date the form was filled which should coincide with the date of enrolment of the patient. 

When to complete this form: This form should be competed each time the patient is interviewed (e.g., at each patient visit or other type of encounter with a health care professional) following commencement of treatment with DTG based regimen.  If the patient is pregnant or becomes pregnant, the Follow up form for pregnant women (Form C) should be completed and linked to other forms. This form should also be completed at post-delivery follow up visits for pregnant women.

Treatment site: Please provide the name of the site where patient is being followed up in the active monitoring program.

Patient ASM Code: Please indicate the patient’s ASM code which is the unique identifier for that patient on this form to enable linking of patients’ previous data. The coding system should be agreed upon and standardized across all sites by the country. If you are completing a Follow up Form for a new-born who has been exposed to DTG based regimen in utero, please provide an ASM code for the new-born and ensure that both the mother’s and the infant’s ID are recorded on the form for proper linkage.

Date of birth and Sex: Record the patient’s date of birth or age and sex as on Form A

Medicines

Provide details of all medicines taken by the patient since the last visit including DTG based regimen. Include start and stop dates for medicines that were started or stopped during the interval since the patient was last interviewed and indicate which medicines continue to be taken. Record the details of any prescription or over-the counter medicines and any traditional medicines, herbal remedies or health supplements taken during this interval. 

Was there any new adverse event after the start of treatment with DTG based regimen

Please indicate if patient experienced any new adverse event after the start of treatment with DTG based regimen. 

If a new adverse event was indicated, please select from the list of possible adverse events  provided or record all new adverse event(s) reported by the patients and provide details of such events. 

Death should be recorded as an event; please also include date and cause of death if known. 

 

If the event was severe, indicate the severity by filling the appropriate code in the space provided. Also indicate if the event was serious or not and the reason for seriousness by selecting the CODES for Severity and Seriousness from the panel as appropriate. èèèèè

Was there worsening of an existing or resurgence of a past medical condition after the start of treatment with DTG based regimen

Please indicate if patient experienced worsening of an existing medical condition or resurgence of a past medical condition after the start of treatment with DTG based regimen. If so, select from the list of conditions provided or record medical conditions not listed that worsened or resurged in the rows marked other.  Indicate if the condition either worsened or resurged by putting the start and stop dates in the appropriate column.

Treatment or action taken to manage adverse event

Indicate treatment or action taken including medicines prescribed to manage any adverse events 

Outcome of adverse event

 

Indicate the Outcome of the adverse event by selecting the CODES for Outcome from the panel as appropriate. èèèèè

Reporter
